About

I am a game designer and developer who mainly focuses on small scale games, mostly styled in 3D, with the occasional 2D.
I mainly focus on survival, simulation, and adventure/metroidvania genres for my games.
I develop and test all the mechanics for the games in house, while most art is currently placeholder art assets that I am using
whilst doing courses and developing the art and graphics myself.

Design

I create and design the mechanics and systems for the game,
documenting the stages of development for easier review.

Development

I develop and implement the mechanics and systems,
using C# in the Unity Engine, following the best code practices.

Launch & Maintenance

I publish all projects on Itch.io,
creating descriptions and adding screenshots of gameplay,
aswell as updating the projects with new content and patches.
